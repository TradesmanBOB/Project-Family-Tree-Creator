Prepare, to grow a tree upwards!

This section is where your family tree will appear once you start adding family members. Use the controls below to begin building your tree. As you add parents, children, or other relatives, the tree will automatically update and display here in a clear, visual format. You can interact with the tree to view details about each person, edit their information, or rearrange relationships as needed. When you're ready, use the "Download as Image" button to save your tree as a picture for sharing or printing. Get started by adding your first family member! First family member must be added as a "root" member or oldest connecting ancestor.
